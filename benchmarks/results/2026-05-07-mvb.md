# Atelier Phase 1 Benchmark — MVB Results

Date: `2026-05-07`

## Method

Observational, not generative. Walks committed code in 4 repos with a `DESIGN.md` token contract and 4 control repos without one. For every color-bearing reference (Tailwind class, inline hex/rgb/hsl), classifies as a **semantic token reference** (e.g. `bg-foreground`, `text-pf`) or a **raw palette reference** (e.g. `bg-zinc-800`, `#1A7BA5`). Conformance is tokens / (tokens + raw).

## Summary

- **DESIGN.md group conformance**: 79.9%
- **Control group conformance**: 39.0%
- **Absolute lift**: +40.9%
- **Relative lift**: +105.1%

## Kill-switch decision

**PROCEED** — 105% relative lift clears the 40% threshold. Ship the public packages.

## Caveats

MVB scope: 4 + 4 repos, observational only. Full Phase 1 would run AI-generation against 30 repos with and without `DESIGN.md` to measure *generation-time* conformance, not just shipped-state conformance. The MVB tests whether the named-handle hypothesis is observable in committed code at all; it does not isolate AI from human contributions.
